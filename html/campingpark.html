Parque de Campismo do Porto Moniz is located at the mouth of Ribeira da Janela and was inaugurated on September 4, 2004. It is about 20 minutes on foot from the village of Porto Moniz and about 4 minutes by car. Area of 5,300m2, while the support building has an area of 450m2. It also has a children's playground.

The Porto Santo Island Campsite is located in Vila Baleira, more precisely in the Fontinha area, next to the beach. It is a wooded and fenced space, with capacity for 800 people. It has changing rooms, TV room, internet, bar and electricity. This space is also capable of receiving Caravans and Motorhomes.
